Never use em dash or double hyphen characters in any UI copy, headings, subheadlines, button text, or descriptions.

**Why:** The user explicitly said they look like AI-generated content ("AI generated content mone hoy").

**How to apply:** Replace separator punctuation with a period, comma, or restructure the sentence. For button text with a price, use `·` (middle dot) or just reorder: "Book Your First Lesson · From $12".
